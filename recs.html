Mel's Movie and TV Show Watchlist

Here's a bunch of reviews of all the movies and TV shows I watched over quarantine!

Disclaimer: This is simply my opinion, so please don't be deterred from watching it yourself (unless I said it's truly awful)
Movies and TV Show Watchlist
Always Be My Maybe

Childhood sweethearts Sasha (Ali Wong) and Marcus (Randall Park) are reunited in San Francisco after not seeing each other for years. Although they live completely different lives, they still have that spark!

Frozen

Three friends are stranded on a ski lift after the ski resort closes for the next five days. And to make matters worse, a winter storm is approaching.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

The Avatar, Aang, masters all these elements. Aang, along with his friends Katara and Sokka, go on this journey to save the world from the power-hungry Fire Nation.

Criminal Minds

A team of FBI profilers go on missions to hunt down criminals by combining each member's expertise to predict the criminal's next steps. The team consists of an experienced profiler, a tech genius, a quirky nerd, and a former media liaison. Each episode is a new crime for this team to solve.

To All the Boys: Always and Forever

Lara Jean and Peter Kavinsky are in their senior year of high school and face the dreaded relationship problem of college plans.

I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

A boy finds a diary of his classmate, who reveals to him that she is suffering from a fatal pancreatic disease that no one knows about.

Your Name

A boy (Taki) and a girl (Mitsuha) share this magical connection where they can swap bodies. However, their memories are always hazy the next day. They decide to meet in person and that's where it really kicks off.
